Chapter 4: Heat

20 Short Questions and Answers

1. What is heat?
Heat is a form of energy that makes an object hot.
2. What is temperature?
Temperature measures the degree of hotness or coldness of a body.
3. What is the SI unit of temperature?
Kelvin (K).
4. What is normal human body temperature?
37째C or 98.6째F.
5. Name the three modes of transfer of heat.
Conduction, Convection, Radiation.
6. In which state does conduction occur?
In solids.
7. In which states does convection occur?
In liquids and gases.
8. Which mode of heat transfer does not require a medium?
Radiation.
9. Give one example of a conductor.
Copper.
10. Give one example of an insulator.
Wood.
11. Why are cooking utensils made of metals?
Because metals are good conductors of heat.
12. Why are gaps left in railway tracks?
To allow expansion during heating.
13. What is sea breeze?
Cool air that moves from sea to land during the day.
14. What is land breeze?
Cool air that moves from land to sea during the night.
15. Which colour absorbs more heat?
Black colour.
16. Why do we wear white clothes in summer?
Because white reflects most of the heat.
17. What happens to liquids on heating?
Liquids expand on heating.
18. What is thermal equilibrium?
When two objects reach the same temperature.
19. What is the range of clinical thermometer?

20. Name one use of heat in daily life.
