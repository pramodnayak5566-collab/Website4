Class 7 CBSE Science

Chapter 4: Heat

50 MCQs with Answers

1. Heat is a form of:
a) Matter
b) Energy
c) Gas
d) Force
Correct Answer: b) Energy
2. The SI unit of temperature is:
a) Celsius
b) Kelvin
c) Fahrenheit
d) Joule
Correct Answer: b) Kelvin
3. Heat flows from:
a) Cold to hot
b) Hot to cold
c) Same temperature
d) Nowhere
Correct Answer: b) Hot to cold
4. Normal body temperature is:
a) 35°C
b) 36°C
c) 37°C
d) 38°C
Correct Answer: c) 37°C
5. Conduction occurs mainly in:
a) Liquids
b) Gases
c) Solids
d) Vacuum
Correct Answer: c) Solids
6. Convection occurs in:
a) Solids
b) Liquids and gases
c) Vacuum
d) Metals only
Correct Answer: b) Liquids and gases
7. Heat from Sun reaches Earth by:
a) Conduction
b) Convection
c) Radiation
d) Evaporation
Correct Answer: c) Radiation
8. A good conductor of heat is:
a) Wood
b) Plastic
c) Copper
d) Rubber
Correct Answer: c) Copper
9. Black colour:
a) Reflects heat
b) Absorbs more heat
c) Produces heat
d) Stops heat
Correct Answer: b) Absorbs more heat
10. Sea breeze occurs during:
a) Night
b) Day
c) Winter
d) Rainy season
Correct Answer: b) Day
